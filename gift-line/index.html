Gift-Line

An applets make the public service activity more fun and attract more people join in buying books for poor kids. Meanwhile, it can enhance the impact of bookstore and become popular.

Background

Xinhua Bookstore is a traditional bookstore with a long history. With the development of the Internet, the channels for people to read have become diversified, and the understanding of bookstores has also changed. Xinhua Bookstore hopes to reorient its development direction through cooperation with Thoughtworks to gain more attention from users and increase their market share. I decided to choose the keyword “public service activity” to try to solve the problem.

How might we help XinHua Bookstore gain more attention through public service activity?
