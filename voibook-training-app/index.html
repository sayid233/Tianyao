Voibook Training App

Listening and Speaking training APP for hearing-loss people. 

Background

I am a hearing-loss person with a cochlear implant. Now I need to practice listening and speaking skills. I have found some software by myself, including Angel Sound,Mandarin Learning and even Chinese proficiency test Apps. Most Mandarin learning apps assume that users can understand Mandarin directly and easily, also the hearing-loss are not their target users.

From a hearing-loss user

There are 3.6 billion hearing-loss people around the world,nearly 90 million in China. The hearing-loss account for more than 30% of the number of disabled people in China. It’s a huge group of people.

Hearing-loss are people whose hearing is partially or completely lost. Hearing-loss people are easily get into troubles in their lives because of the adverse effects of hearing on them. So they can’t get into mainstream society very well. In fact, people with hearing impairment have the same vocal organs as normal people. Therefore, as long as they get suitable training, the hearing-loss people can possibly speak in the future.

We attempted to design a solution that would help hearing-loss people do listening and speaking rehabilitation. We framed out design challenge as follows:

How might we help hearing-loss people do listening and speaking rehabilitation through APP?

We followed a process with 3 phases of research: exploratory, generative, and evaluative. As a result, we were able to experiment with a variety of research methods, which led to some valuable insights and concept directions.

Exploratory Research

Literature Review

I began by reading a lot of thesis about characteristics, language recognition and rehabilitation of the hearing-loss. Through this research I discovered some initial insights that guided us moving forward:

  • Although people with hearing impairment can gain certain phonological awareness through lip reading, language training and hearing aids, their voice ability is still much lower than normal people.
  • Listening and Speaking are both important for language rehabilitation.
  • Hearing-loss people are better at using visual and kinesthetic cognition.
Competitive Analysis

Most of the current foreign apps are developed by speech therapy companies, and many applications are designed for speech language therapists. Foreign apps will classify vowels, consonants, and consonant clusters. their target is clear, and most of them have different difficult degree. In addition, there are also APPs dedicated to a certain consonant. Most domestic apps are designed for children to learn pinyin. There are no apps for both hearing and speech rehabilitation.

I also learned some devices for speech rehabilitation, such as Changzhou Qianxi, Shanghai Taiyige and COGNI. Those devices are expensive and mostly designed for school, hospital and language rehabilitation institution. I tried COGNI, actually the design and content are simple. Many features can be fully applied in apps.

User Interview

To understand the problem scope and the exercises experience from both sides, I conducted interviews with 5 hearing-loss people and concluded several points.

  • In general, they need hearing persons' help to correct their pronunciation
  • Users have a greater need for voice correction
  • They hope the App can tell them how to improve their pronunciation

Evaluative Research

With our concept direction set, I began creating wireframes. I developed various iterations of the key features and began gathering feedback from our expert and development teams.

Wireframing

I received vital feedback from our team that we then integrated into our final designs.

  • If it is just a large number of cards sliding, it is difficult for users to locate the content they want to practice, which is less efficient.
  • In the listening part, the user wants to return to the original question page to listen the difference after they did multiple choice questions.
  • Users want to see their progress when they did training. 

Voibook Training App

The result of all this research and iteration is the Listening and Speaking training APP for hearing-loss people. Voibook training App is in order to help hearing-loss people to do basic language rehabilitation and live a normal life.
