Shopee Redesign

This case study will analyse in detail and aim to improve the user experience that Shopee buyers have when using the app to find and choose products.

Design Process

Discover

In order to find an UX issue of shopee, I drew wireframe to understand each part of the APP. I want to focus on the most important flow of shopee——Finding and Choosing products.

Pain Point

  • The main page contains too much information and kind confusing
  • Hard to find categories
  • Difficult to touch the shopping cart
  • The filtering keyword is nice
User Testing

I invited 2 users and ask them to finish a task in 3 Apps——Shopee, Lazada and Qoo10.They speak loudly of what they are thinking during the test. I am observing their operations and ask how they feel after the test.    

Task : Now you are gonna buy a gift to your friend’s cat, but you are not familiar with pet stuff, then how would you learn different products for pets? Try to choose a gift in such situation.

Pain Point of Shopee

  • They can’t find where is the categories at first
  • They can’t find where is the shopping cart when they are ready to pay
User Comments

I tried to search comments from users. Many users are complaining about the search engine and the system of shopee. I try to find the complaints that I can improve through design.

Pain Point

  • Hard to navigate
  • Crowded with pop-ups about deals
Competitive Analysis

After I searched a lot of information about southeast asian e-commerce, I found that Shopee, Lazada, Carousell, ZALORA and Qoo10 are popular, so I downloaded them to analyse them. In order to understand the user flow of finding and choosing products in different apps, I drew wireframes of each app to anslyse that.
