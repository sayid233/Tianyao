02 Strawberry

An App to help couples maintain their long distance relationship through artificial intelligence.

Background

I was very happy talking on the phone one second before, but when I hang up the phone, my tears ran down my cheek.

From a girl in long distance relationship

Nowadays, more and more couples have to leave each other and live in two different cities because of various reasons such as studying or working. Therefore, long distance relationships can cause a lot of trouble. We want to design an App to help couples maintain their long distance relationship somehow. We framed out design challenge as follows:

How might we use artificial intelligence to help couples in long-distance relationship enhance their connection?

We followed a process with 3 phases of research: exploratory, generative, and evaluative. As a result, we were able to experiment with a variety of research methods, which led to some valuable insights and concept directions.

Exploratory Research

Brainstorming
Literature Review

I began by reading some thesis about long-distance relationship. Through this research I discovered some initial insights that guided us moving forward:

Characteristics of long-distance couples

  • High economic pressure on both sides
  • Excessive expectations for face-to-face communication
  • Need more emotional experience than ordinary couples

How to maintain long-distance relationship

  • Long-distance is a common thing
  • Use different communication methods: send gifts, videos, recordings
  • Discuss some profound questions together: Will we date someone else?
  • Trust and honesty
  • Have a positive attitude
