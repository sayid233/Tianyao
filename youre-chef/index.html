You’re Chef

Website designed to make busy life easier and improve the life quality through food packages

Background

As a student, I’m often looking out for opportunities to gain some experience conceptualizing and designing applications end-to-end. When I was studying abroad in America, I found it’s really difficult to decide what to cook everyday. I usually spent 2 hours on preparing food and cooking, but it only takes me 5 minutes to eat. After that, I have to do the dishes. I love cooking, because it’s healthy and cheap. However, I just feel too tired to cook after studying at most time. I feel like it’s a common problem for all the young people.

I attempted to design a solution that would help customers have fun in the kitchen and learn to cook delicious meals. I framed out design challenge as follows:

How might we solve the problem that people always don’t know what to eat everyday?

Exploratory Research

Competitive Analysis

I found that surprisingly there is no food box subscription service in China. So I analysed several famous food box subscription websites in America. there are a lot of different kinds of food boxes, such as grocery box(ingredients), pre-made food box, baking box, snack box and so on.

The good thing is that they are convenient and healthy. They want their customers to enjoy food. They also have special menu considered for vegetarian and people who want to keep fit. Mostly customers can choose what they want, but those choices are limited. It seems most food boxes are designed for two people or more, but actually people who live alone also need such service. The reason is they can’t buy a lot of food at one time and usually it’s kind difficult to cook the right amount for one person.
