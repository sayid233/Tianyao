Disappearing Island

From this project, We wanna show the importance of protecting our environment through information visualization in 3 interactive installations.

Glacier is melting

The first one is an oil painting. At first, it shows antarctic glaciers and cute penguins. Then everyone can draw something on the painting by using blue. The oil paint is like sea-water,  it gradually submerges the land because of global warming. 

Disappearing island

The second one is the disappearing island. In the beginning, We put ice cubes on the pot and when ice cubes melted, the simulated sea-water will submerge the island. 

The future of humanity

The third one is letting people be under the projection and feel the sound and vision of sea-water.
