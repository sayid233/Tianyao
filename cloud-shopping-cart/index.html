07 Cloud Shopping Cart

Background

As a college student, I almost buy all the things online. And when I mentioned what I bought  with my roommates or when my roommates see things I bought, they would ask me for the link. Since we always shop in the same store online, I found it’s tiresome to wait for my roommates to choose and it’s complicated to split the money, especially there is a coupon. Also, it’s easy to forget to pay back to the person who paid all the stuff.

So I want to design a function module of an APP to solve the problem and improve the user experience of people who have roommates and like to shop together online. I framed out design challenge as follows:

How might we help roommates shop online together and split the bill easily?

Exploratory Research

User Journey Map

I mapped out a user journey of a typical buy-things-together process to visualize areas where pain-points might arise.

User Interview

To understand the problem scope and the dining experience from both sides, I conducted 5 interviews with 4 students and 1 work starter.

  • Shopping togethter mostly happened during big sales
  • Users usually spent a lot of time on waiting their friends to decide what to buy
  • Girls like to discuss about goods and share to each other
  • It’s complicated to calculate（eg：$20 off $75 purchase）
Competitive Analysis

I observed several money split apps that help users to calculate money and split bill. Also,I found out some similar functions from Uber, Meituan and Airbnb.
